  <h3>About Us</h3>

    <p>Afghan Process Company, formed in 2014, is an Afghan agri-business company that specializes in the agricultural/horticultural development of Afghanistan's raisin production

and processing sector.</p>

    <p>We are Afghanistan’s only private social-ethical company that is involved in the provision of bringing not only the latest technology and knowledge to Afghanistan’s raisin production sector but just as importantly applying international food safety standards and

requirements in the production, processing and exporting of Afghan raisins.</p>

    <p>By value and volume, grapes and raisins are the biggest perennial fruit crop in Afghanistan and provide the most income for farmers, and historically, export capital for Afghanistan.</p>

    <p>We are not a venture capitalist firm that is all profit. We are a proud social-ethical company

with a unique ethical business model and culture that goes beyond the standard ethical

compliance programs, notions of corporate responsibility, fashionable environmental

policies and basic community involvement. Our business is to create socially sustainable and

ethical produce, not only for the end-buyer but for the local community, to provide them

with the means to improve their livelihoods and have the means for the basic human needs

      such as healthcare, education and social security.</p>

    <p>Our modern factory is equipped with all the latest processing technology consisting of a pre-processing line, a wash line with laser sorting, a dryer line and finally a packing line.</p>
